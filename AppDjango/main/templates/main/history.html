End Game May
2023
Final part

In the final month of development, we added highly anticipated online multiplayer functionality, enhancing the social aspect of the game. We also focused on improving the visual appeal by introducing various in-game decorations. Additionally, new captivating entities were introduced to make the gameplay even more engaging. Finally, we successfully launched the game's official website, serving as a central hub for information and community interaction. These updates showcase our commitment to delivering an immersive gaming experience that surpasses our previous achievements.

Improvement March
2023
Improvement Phase

During the Improvement Phase, our primary focus was on implementing multiplayer functionality and enhancing the user experience through additions such as music and room decorations.

Moreover, this phase also included the final development of our website, adding necessary elements to showcase our game and provide detailed information about the development process.

February
2023 Developpement
Development Phase

During the development phase, we applied all the previously designed object-oriented classes to create a beta version of our game, as well as developing our website.The game features rooms, objects to find, and an engaging backstory.

This period was focused on bringing together all aspects of the game and website, providing a more cohesive experience for our users.

2023 Graphic Engine

During this sprint, we focused on object-oriented design, carefully considering the creation, utility, and organization of classes to make the app as optimized as possible.

This approach allowed us to address various aspects of video game development efficiently and maintainably.
